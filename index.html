Retention Features

Every pin is cold-formed with precision retention features engineered for your specific application requirements.

Star

Highest retention and anti-rotational strength. Diameter up to 50% greater than wire. Ideal for circuit boards.

Tabs / Spline

Strong retention with minimal diameter expansion. Best for post-insertion into molded plastic or pressed metal.

Inscribed Square

Retention without protrusion. Ideal for high-speed automation where pins slide down tracks without orientation.

Flat

Maximum anti-pullout and anti-rotational force. Often oriented 90° from each other on opposite ends.
