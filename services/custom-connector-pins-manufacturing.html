Cold-Form Process

Automatic Die Rolling Technology

Our cold-form manufacturing process pushes raw material into a custom die and forces it to conform to the die shape. No material is cut away, so there is zero scrap. No heat is generated, preserving the strength and integrity of every pin. And because the die does all the shaping, there is no retooling between runs.

  • Zero scrap - no material waste whatsoever
  • No heat generation - material properties preserved
  • No retooling between production runs
  • Lead-free brass and stainless steel available
