Progressive Die Engineering

Metal Flows, Not Cuts

After DFM design refinement, Electropin's experts create the custom tools and dies needed to cold-form your connector pins to exact specifications. Progressive dies are developed for the automatic die rolling process. As raw material rolls through the die, it is shaped by flow rather than removal -- no material is ever cut away.

  • Progressive dies for automatic die rolling
  • Metal shaped by flow, not removal
  • Custom tooling to your exact specifications
  • Fully integrated with DFM consultation

How the Die Rolling Process Works

  1. 1
    After DFM consultation, the pin design is finalized with all dimensional and functional requirements confirmed.
  2. 2
    Electropin's tool and die experts engineer a progressive die set specific to your pin geometry.
  3. 3
    Raw material (lead-free brass or stainless steel) is fed into the die rolling machine.
  4. 4
    As the material rolls through progressive stations, it is shaped by the dies -- metal flows into the specified form without any cutting.
  5. 5
    The finished pin exits with all retention features formed, ready for quality inspection and delivery.
