Retention Feature Types

Choose the right retention feature for your application. Our DFM experts can help you select the optimal solution.

Star

Highest retention and anti-rotational strength. ~50% diameter expansion. Ideal for circuit boards and high-stress applications.

Tabs / Spline

Strong retention with minimal expansion. Best for plastic and metal inserts where tight tolerances on housing dimensions are critical.

Inscribed Square

Retention without protrusion beyond the pin diameter. Ideal for automation and applications requiring a clean, compact profile.

Flat

Maximum anti-pullout force with 90-degree orientation. Ensures the pin stays securely seated in the most demanding applications.

Choosing the Right Feature

  1. 1
    Star -- Choose when you need the highest retention and anti-rotational strength. The ~50% diameter expansion provides maximum holding power, making stars ideal for circuit board applications.
  2. 2
    Tabs / Spline -- Choose when housing dimensions are tight and minimal expansion is critical. Best for plastic and metal inserts where the pin must not significantly enlarge the receiving hole.
  3. 3
    Inscribed Square -- Choose when the pin must not protrude beyond its nominal diameter. Ideal for automated assembly processes where a clean, compact profile is required.
  4. 4
    Flat -- Choose when maximum anti-pullout force is the priority. The 90-degree orientation provides secure seating in applications with high axial loads.
